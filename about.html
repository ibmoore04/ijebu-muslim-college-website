About Us

Excellence, Discipline, Faith — nurturing minds since 1962

Our History

Founded in 1950 by community leaders and Islamic scholars, Ijebu Muslim College was established to provide quality Western education without compromising Islamic values. What began as a small community school has grown into one of Ogun State's most respected educational institutions.

Over six decades, we have produced thousands of graduates who have become doctors, engineers, lawyers, academics, and business leaders across Nigeria and globally. Our alumni network remains active and committed to giving back to their alma mater.

Today, the college sits on a sprawling 50-acre campus with modern classrooms, science laboratories, a library, ICT center, and sports facilities — all while maintaining our core Islamic identity.

Our Mission

To provide affordable, quality education rooted in Islamic principles, empowering students with leadership skills, moral character, and academic excellence to succeed in a competitive global environment.

Our Vision

To be a global centre of excellence producing God-conscious leaders who contribute positively to humanity, uphold integrity, and drive innovation in their chosen fields.
