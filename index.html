Our History

Founded in 1962, Ijebu Muslim College blends Western education with Islamic ethics. We stand as a premier institution in Ogun State, producing scholars, doctors, engineers, and leaders across Nigeria and the globe.

Our alumni network spans 6 decades of excellence with state-of-the-art facilities.
